Our Mission

A Trust Built on
Human Dignity

We believe every individual — regardless of where they were born — deserves the opportunity to flourish. Valam Trust partners with communities, not just for them, building lasting, self-sustaining change.

  • Quality Education
    Bridging learning gaps for 20,000+ children through schools, digital classrooms, and tutoring networks.
  • Community Healthcare
    Mobile clinics and health camps bringing primary care to 180+ remote villages every month.
  • Sustainable Livelihoods
    Skill-building and microfinance programs creating 5,000+ sustainable jobs for rural families.

Who We Are

Fifteen Years of
Purpose-Driven Action

Founded in 2009, Valam Trust has grown from a small local initiative into one of Tamil Nadu's most trusted non-profits, serving 52,000+ lives across 18 districts.

Our Journey

History & Legacy

2009
Foundation Established
Valam Trust registered in Chennai by founder Anandhi Krishnamurthy with a vision to bridge the rural-urban opportunity gap across Tamil Nadu.
2011
First Major Grant
Received ₹25L seed grant from Tata Trusts, enabling launch of our first 10 community learning centres.
2014
Healthcare Wing Launched
Expanded scope to mobile health clinics and maternal care, initially covering 3 districts.
2017
80G & FCRA Certification
Achieved FCRA registration and 80G status, enabling international and tax-exempt domestic donations.
2020
COVID Relief Operations
Distributed 1.2L food kits and set up 8 oxygen support centres across Tamil Nadu during the pandemic.
2024
52,000 Lives Milestone
Celebrated 15 years of impact, crossing 52,000 direct beneficiaries and ₹12Cr total funds utilised.
